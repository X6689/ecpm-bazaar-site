CSV templates

Prepare ad monetization data for diagnosis.

These templates help small mobile game and app teams compare the latest day with the previous day by revenue, weighted eCPM, impressions, fill rate, country, placement, and ad source. You can anonymize app names before using the demo.

Required fields

Minimum data needed for a useful diagnosis

dateReport date. Use YYYY-MM-DD when possible.
appNameGame or app name. This can be anonymized.
placementNameAd unit, placement, or ad format name.
countryCountry code or country name.
networkAd source, mediation platform, or aggregate source.
revenueEstimated ad revenue in USD.
impressionsAd impressions for the row.

Recommended fields

Better fields for finding the real driver

ecpmRevenue / impressions * 1000. eCPM Bazaar can calculate it if missing.
requestsAd requests. Needed for fill-rate diagnosis.
fillsMatched or filled requests. Needed for fill-rate diagnosis.
clicksOptional, useful for CTR sanity checks.

Privacy first

The public demo parses CSV files in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or stored. For manual review, remove app IDs, exact app names, and any private account identifiers before sending data.

AdMob revenue drop

Why did my AdMob revenue drop?

A revenue drop is only the final symptom. Before changing floors, SDKs, or mediation settings, split the drop into traffic, fill, pricing, and mix changes.

Check in this order

  1. Did impressions or requests drop first?
  2. Did match rate, fill rate, or show rate move?
  3. Did traffic shift by country or device?
  4. Did one ad unit, placement, or format change?
  5. Did one ad source or mediation source stop filling?
  6. Did time-of-day behavior, holidays, or live events reduce peak-hour impressions?
  7. Did eCPM fall after the upstream metrics stayed stable?

How to read it

Common interpretation

If impressions and fill are stable but eCPM dropped, the issue may be demand or auction pricing. If fill or impressions moved first, the revenue drop is probably not an eCPM problem.

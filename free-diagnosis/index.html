Send anonymized ad monetization data for a free diagnosis.

If your mobile game or app ad revenue changed and you are not sure why, send anonymized CSV-style rows. I will help check whether the movement looks more like eCPM, impressions, fill rate, country mix, placement, or ad source performance.

1. Anonymize your data

Replace app names, ad unit IDs, and account identifiers with neutral names such as Game A or Rewarded Home.

2. Include two comparable periods

The simplest version is latest day vs previous day. A 7-day comparison is also useful if your traffic is volatile.

3. Send fields, not account access

CSV rows are enough for early diagnosis. Do not send passwords, login sessions, API keys, or private account screenshots.

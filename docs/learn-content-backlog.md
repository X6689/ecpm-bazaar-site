## Publishing rules

- Do not create a separate guide where an existing guide already has the same primary intent.
- Use `most likely driver`, `supporting signals`, `recommended checks`, and `data limitations` rather than claiming a confirmed cause.
- Keep match rate and fill rate separate unless the source report proves the definitions are the same.
- Use weighted eCPM from total revenue divided by total impressions; never average row-level eCPM values.
- Add each published guide to `public/sitemap.xml` and include at least two relevant internal links.

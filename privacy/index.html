Data safety

Use anonymized ad monetization data. Keep account access private.

eCPM Bazaar is being validated with privacy-first workflows: browser-only CSV parsing, anonymized rows, and no account credentials.

Browser-only public demo

CSV files selected in the public demo are parsed in your browser. They are not uploaded or stored by eCPM Bazaar.

No credentials required

Do not send AdMob, AppLovin MAX, Unity LevelPlay, TopOn, Google, or mediation login credentials. The early workflow only needs anonymized report rows.

Anonymize before sharing

Replace app names, ad unit IDs, account IDs, package names, and any private identifiers before sending rows for manual review.

Diagnosis, not financial advice

The output is a practical diagnosis to guide investigation. It is not a guarantee of revenue improvement or a recommendation to change business settings blindly.
